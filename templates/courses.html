Courses

I am an experienced trainer and lecturer. You can book me for corporate trainings or university courses that guide your participants towards becoming experienced practitioners. By default, trainings contain substantial practical exercises.

I believe the best setting for effective training is in-person. You are welcome to host trainings in your own rooms. I also have my own training room in Berlin-Schöneberg for groups of up to 8 persons. It is fitted with a projector, flipchart and lots of equipment for a creative training experience.

Software Engineering

Since my early days of programming in the 1990's, I have seen a fair bit of software engineering. No matter whether you write in Python or Java, whether you use SQL or NoSQL, in the end everything comes down to finding a good abstraction and creating conditions where maintainable code can thrive. I am available to support projects as a part-time senior developer. Here are a couple of things that I have enjoyed in past projects:

  • Refactoring legacy code
  • Improving CI pipelines
  • Creating test harnesses
  • Requirements engineering
  • Making sense of vibe-coded prototypes
